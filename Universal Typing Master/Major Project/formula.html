Q. What WPM (Words Per Minute) is considered good ?

Ans. Typically 40+ WPM is considered good while An average professional typist types usually in speeds
        of 50-80 WPM.

Q. How much time usually it takes to reach 40+ WPM for an absolute beginner ?

Ans. It usually takes 1 to 1.5 month of practice to cross 40+ WPM mark with daily 30 minutes of practice.
       But it also depends on the individual's capability.
